Our Curated Library

By Brandon and Nicole B)

Hello! This is Brandon and Nicole's curated library of 11 video game soundtracks that we love and enjoy listening to. We believe that video game music is much more powerful than some would like to believe, and a game's music can powerfully change the theme and feel of a video game. Some of these soundtracks can be seen as having simple instrumentation, but some have very thoughtfully chosen orchestration and instrumentation. Enjoy listening!
