This website was created for the class Photos and Words, DM-UY 3183.

Nicole Zhao is a student at NYU Tandon School of Engineering studying Integrated Design and Media. She considers Staten Island to be home and is inspired to preserve its rich history through her work.

The photographs were taken from February to March 2022 with the intention of documenting the state of Midland Beach as construction of the Bluebelt Program wraps up.
