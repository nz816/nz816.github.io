Ineligible to Vote?
Just because you can't vote doesn't mean you can't make an impact!
Here are some ways you can make your voice heard!
  1. Register people to vote!

    2. Get your family, friends, neighbors, etc. registered to vote! By having more people registered, we strengthen our democracy.

  2. Help people make their voting plan.

    3. By helping people make their voting plan, they are more likely to actually vote! Ask people how they're planning to vote, where they're planning to vote, and when they are planning to vote.

  3. Reach out to your local government representatives.

    4. As a resident of your district, you are still a constituent even if you can't vote! Local representatives want to hear about your ideas to make your community better.

  4. Volunteer!

    5. Find a cause that matters to you! Phone bank or canvas for your local representative! The most impactful campaigns grow from grassroot fundraising and community awareness.
